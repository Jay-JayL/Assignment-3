Carl Sassenrath



Carl Sassenrath is known for giving the World its first ever multitasking personal computers.

Here is a quote from his website

Back in 1985 I was lucky enough to introduce multitasking to the world of personal computers with the Commodore Amiga Operating System Kernel.

- Carl Sassenrath went to the Univerisity of California.

- In 1979, he was recruited as an opertating system engineer.

- In 1983, Sassenrath joined Amiga where he developped the first multitasking personal computer.

- Later on, he joined Apple and left Silicon Valley in 1988.
